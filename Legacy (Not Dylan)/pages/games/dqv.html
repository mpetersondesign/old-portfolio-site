Dragon Quest V -- Story

From the Hero's birth to his kids', you follow the story of a young boy who accompanies his father Pankraz across the globe in search of his mother. Through their travels, he meets many companions and sees his father off to a castle in the east. When the prince of the castle gets kidnapped, the young hero and Pankraz give chase to the thieves, ending up in a confrontation with the powerful dark sorceror Ladja. After the hero and the prince watch as Ladja eradicates Pankraz, the fiend captures them both, putting them into slavery for ten long years...

After a lengthy jailbreak, the prince and the now matured hero escape their captivity, floating safely to shore as grown men. Setting out to avenge his father as the prince settles down for the good of his kingdom, the hero will once again cross paths with his old friends, settle down, have kids, and eventually save the world.

Dragon Quest V -- History

Dragon Quest V (The second game to have the same title in both Japan and America) released in 1992, having a superior, fully 3D rendered remake on the PS2 in 2004, and another 2D remaster on the DS in 2008. Dragon Quest V is perhaps the most popular game in the series based on its expansive story alone, being the only Dragon Quest game to get its own feature-length film in 2019. (though we don't talk about that)


Dragon Quest V came in 11th in the Famitsu "Top 100 Games of all time", earning its spot in JRPG history. Coincidentally, Dragon Quest V is also Yuji Horii's favorite in the series. The 2011 Japanese Comedy series "The Hero Yoshihiko and the Demon King's Castle" is HEAVILY based on the characters and mannerisms of Dragon Quest V.

Dragon Quest V -- Gameplay

Dragon Quest V not only allows you to have pre-set party members in your company, but you have a chance to even recruit monsters in your party after a battle. You can have up to 36-40 different monsters join your party during the course of the game depending on which version you choose. Monsters have their own resistances and special spells to help the party out even more than your human companions.
