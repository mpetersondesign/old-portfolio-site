List of Dragon Quest spin-off games


Much like other popularized franchises, Dragon Quest has spawned a number of spin-off titles, proving popular in the market. Dragon Quest spin-off titles, such as the Joker series (Released in the US, no less.) are very popular in Japan, spawning as many as 3 sequels for a single spin off.
